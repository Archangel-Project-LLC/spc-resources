Shopping list guide

The staples worth keeping in the house. Shop the protein and veg sections first — if those are covered, the week takes care of itself. Tick items off as you go; your list resets when you reload after a clear.

Buying smart

Frozen veg and frozen fish are nutritionally excellent, cheaper, and don't go off mid-week — keep the freezer stocked. Buy mince and chicken in bulk when reduced, portion it, and freeze.

Pre-cooked rice pouches, tinned fish and rotisserie chicken are legitimate tools, not cheating. The best plan is the one you'll actually execute on a busy Tuesday.

Tracking isn't forever — it's a skill, so you can eventually eyeball portions with confidence.
