Your coaching roster, synced to the cloud and available on every device you sign in from. Leads, active clients, check-in dates and notes — all in one place.

Finish setup — about 5 minutes, once

This app stores your clients in your own free Supabase database so they sync across devices. Connect it once and you're done.

  1. Create a free project at supabase.com (any region near you). Give it a minute to spin up.
  2. Open the project's SQL Editor, paste in the contents of supabase-schema.sql from this repo, and click Run.
  3. Go to Project Settings → API. Copy the Project URL and the anon public key.
  4. Paste both into the CONFIG block near the top of crm.html, then save/commit.
  5. In Authentication → Sign In / Providers, make sure Email is enabled. Reload this page and create your account.
