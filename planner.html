Weekly meal planner

Three meals a day, protein and veg in every one. Note your training in the last column and put your biggest carb meals around it. Your plan saves automatically on this device, or print a blank copy for the fridge.

How to use it

Build each meal in this order: protein first (palm-sized minimum), veg second (half the plate), then carbs scaled to training — more on hard sessions, less on rest days. Fats mostly take care of themselves through cooking and protein sources.

Repeat meals you enjoy. Variety across the week matters more than variety across the day, and repeated meals are the easiest ones to track.

Tracking isn't forever — it's a skill, so you can eventually eyeball portions with confidence.
